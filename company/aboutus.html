Our Journey

The Verifitech Story

From a small startup to a global verification leader

2010

The Beginning

Founded in Bangalore with a vision to automate manual background verification processes. First office: a 200 sq ft shared space.

2013

Tech Breakthrough

Launched India's first AI-powered document verification engine. Secured Series A funding of $5M to scale operations.

2016

Global Expansion

Opened offices in Singapore and Dubai. Expanded verification capabilities to 15 countries. Achieved ISO 27001 certification.

2019

Enterprise Dominance

Crossed 1,000 enterprise clients. Launched real-time criminal database monitoring. Acquired two regional competitors.

2023

AI Revolution

Introduced predictive risk analytics using machine learning. Processed 50 millionth verification. Expanded to US and UK markets.

2026

Industry Leader

2,000+ clients across 25 countries. Recognized as Gartner Leader in Background Verification. Continuing to innovate.
