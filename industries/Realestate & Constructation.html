Industry Challenges

Why Construction Needs Rigorous Screening

High-Risk Work Environments

Construction sites rank among the most dangerous workplaces with fatal injury rates 3x the national average. Unqualified workers, expired certifications, and safety violations create liability exposure, project delays, and potential criminal negligence charges for employers.

Complex Compliance Requirements

OSHA, EPA, DOT, and state licensing boards impose strict requirements. Contractor fraud, unlicensed work, and expired certifications can void insurance, trigger stop-work orders, and expose developers to millions in liability and project delays.

Property Access & Trust

Property managers and real estate agents hold keys to occupied homes, handle tenant deposits, and manage sensitive financial data. Background checks prevent theft, unauthorized access, and protect residents from potentially dangerous individuals.
