Industry Challenges

Why Transport Needs Specialized Screening

Regulatory Complexity

DOT, FMCSA, and state regulations create a complex compliance landscape. Violations result in CSA score impacts, fines, and out-of-service orders. Non-compliant drivers put operating authority at risk and expose carriers to nuclear verdicts.

Driver Shortage Crisis

With 80,000+ driver shortage, carriers face pressure to hire quickly. Fast screening must balance speed with thoroughness. One bad hire can cost $15,000+ in turnover, accidents, or compliance violations. Verify before you hire.

Cargo & Facility Security

Warehouse staff and drivers handle high-value cargo, access secure facilities, and cross international borders. Theft, smuggling, and security breaches cost the industry billions. Background checks protect supply chain integrity.

CDL Driver Vetting

Continuous Monitoring

Real-time MVR monitoring, CSA score tracking, and violation alerts. Know immediately when drivers receive tickets, fail drug tests, or have license changes. Proactive compliance management prevents surprises and reduces risk.

Warehouse & Distribution

Screening for forklift operators, pickers, packers, and distribution managers. Equipment certification verification, safety training records, and criminal background checks for personnel handling inventory and operating machinery.

Cross-Border & TWIC

Specialized screening for port workers, hazmat haulers, and international drivers. TWIC card verification, hazmat endorsement checks, FAST card validation, and customs compliance for secure supply chain operations.

DOT Drug Testing

FMCSA-compliant drug and alcohol testing programs. Pre-employment screening, random testing pools, post-accident testing, and return-to-duty programs. Maintain compliance and safety with certified labs and medical review officers.

Owner-Operator Verification

Independent contractor screening for lease operators and fleet owners. Verify operating authority, insurance coverage, safety ratings, and equipment compliance. Protect your brokerage from co-employment and liability issues.
